Saveetha University core Project

2024 - 2025

This project focuses on developing a Convolutional Neural Network (ConvNet) deep learning model to detect and identify anatomical landmarks on vertebrae from medical imaging data, such as X-rays or MRIs. The goal is to improve the accuracy and efficiency of spinal assessments, which are critical in diagnosing and treating various spinal conditions. By training the model on a large dataset of labeled images, the system learns to recognize specific vertebral landmarks with high precision. This automated approach reduces the need for manual annotation by radiologists, saving time and minimizing human error. Ultimately, the project aims to enhance clinical workflows and support better patient outcomes in spinal healthcare.

Saveetha University Portal

November 2022

This project aims to develop an automated reminder system for the Saveetha College ARMS (Attendance and Records Management System) portal. The system sends timely message reminders to Parents regarding their attendance status, helping them stay informed about their attendance records and avoid potential academic issues due to absenteeism. By integrating with the existing ARMS portal, the reminder system fetches attendance data and delivers personalized notifications via SMS . This ensures students are consistently aware of their attendance obligations, promoting better attendance management. The project enhances student engagement and accountability within the academic environment.

AI with IoT

March 2023

This project involves creating an AI-based system to detect gas levels in LPG cylinders and automate the booking process for refills. Using sensors to monitor the gas levels in real-time, the system employs machine learning algorithms to predict when the gas will run out. Once a low gas level is detected, the system automatically places an order for a new cylinder through the integrated booking platform. This ensures a continuous supply of LPG without manual intervention, enhancing convenience and safety for users. The project aims to streamline the refill process and prevent the inconvenience of running out of gas unexpectedly.
